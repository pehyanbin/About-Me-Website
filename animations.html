Implemented Animations

Matrix Rain Effect

A JavaScript-powered background animation that simulates the digital rain effect from "The Matrix".

This animation is active on all pages, including About, Hometown, Achievements, Casual, and Academic.

Title Text Animations

The bounceRotate animation is applied to page and section titles to make them pop. It starts small and invisible, quickly scales up past its final size, and then settles into place.

You can see this on the main title of the About Me page..

Page Load Animations

Several animations are used to make the page content load in a visually appealing way:

  • Header & Nav Bar: The slideDown animation makes the header and navigation bar slide down from the top of the screen when the page loads.
  • Content Containers: The main content cards use fadeIn2 to fade in and slide up from below, drawing attention to the content.
  • Navigation Links: The links in the navigation bar use fadeIn to appear with a slight delay and upward motion.

Hover Effects and Transitions

Smooth CSS transitions are used to provide feedback when you interact with elements:

  • Navigation Links: Hovering over a link in the navigation bar causes it to scale up and change background color.
  • Profile Picture: On the About Me page, the profile picture has a continuous pulseProfile animation. Hovering over it makes it grow larger, rotate slightly, and glow.
  • Cards: The info cards on the About Me page change background color on hover for a responsive feel.
  • Header: Hovering over the header in the webpages causes the text to pop up with a shadow effect like a 3d model. It has a delay of 0.7 seconds so to ensure that the animation only loads when the page is fully loaded. This effect is also applied to all the other webpages as well.
